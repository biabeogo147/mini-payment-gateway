\chapter{Refund API}

Refund APIs are merchant HMAC APIs. The MVP implements full refunds only.
Partial refunds are rejected by design.

\section{\apiid{REF-01} Create Refund}

\toprule

Method and path & \code{POST /v1/refunds} \\
Actor and auth & Merchant backend with HMAC headers. \\
Key request fields & \code{refund_id}, \code{refund_amount}, \code{reason}, exactly one of \code{original_transaction_id} or \code{order_id}. \\
Key response fields & \code{refund_transaction_id}, \code{original_transaction_id}, \code{refund_id}, \code{refund_amount}, \code{refund_status}. \\
Important business rules & Merchant must be \code{ACTIVE}; original payment must be \code{SUCCESS}; refund amount must equal the original payment amount; refund window is seven days from \code{paid_at}; duplicate \code{refund_id} is idempotent per merchant. \\
Important error cases & \code{MERCHANT_NOT_ACTIVE}, \code{PAYMENT_NOT_REFUNDABLE}, \code{REFUND_WINDOW_EXPIRED}, \code{REFUND_AMOUNT_NOT_FULL}, \code{REFUND_NOT_ALLOWED}. \\
Related scenarios & \code{REF-01}, \code{REF-06}, \code{REF-07}, \code{REF-08}, \code{REF-09}. \\
