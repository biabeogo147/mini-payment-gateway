\chapter{Outbound Webhook Contract}

Outbound webhook delivery is a gateway-to-merchant contract. The Mini Payment
Gateway creates events when payment or refund final states change. Delivery is
asynchronous and does not control the final payment or refund state.

Important business rules & HTTP 2xx marks delivery success; non-2xx, timeout, and network errors are failed attempts; retry schedule is 1, 5, and 15 minutes before final failure on attempt 4. \\
Important error cases & Delivery failure does not roll back final payment or refund state. \\
