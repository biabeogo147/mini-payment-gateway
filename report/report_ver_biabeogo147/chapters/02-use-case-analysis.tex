\chapter{Use-Case Analysis}

This chapter describes the actors that interact with the Mini Payment Gateway
and the main use cases the system supports. The focus is the business role of
each actor and the expected system outcome.

\section{Actor Overview}

\begin{table}[!htbp]
\centering
\caption{Implemented actors}
\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{L{0.25\textwidth}Y}
\toprule
\textbf{Actor} & \textbf{Role in the system} \\
\midrule
Admin & Privileged internal user who manages internal accounts, merchant
readiness, merchant credentials, VietQR receiving accounts, and merchant
dashboard accounts. \\
\bodyrowrule
Ops & Internal operations user who monitors transactions, configures QR
receiving accounts, investigates delivery issues, reviews evidence, and
performs allowed recovery actions, including provisioning and supporting
merchant dashboard accounts. \\
\bodyrowrule
Merchant backend & Merchant-owned application that creates payments and refunds
through the gateway on behalf of the merchant business. \\
\bodyrowrule
Merchant dashboard user & Human merchant representative who signs in to inspect
transactions, analytics, profile information, and credential metadata. \\
\bodyrowrule
Provider callback source & External payment-result source that signs callback
messages and confirms payment and refund outcomes. \\
\bodyrowrule
Scheduled trigger & Time-based trigger that activates expiration handling and
webhook retry processing. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabularx}
\end{table}

\section{Core Gateway Use Cases}

\begin{longtable}{L{0.11\textwidth}L{0.25\textwidth}L{0.18\textwidth}L{0.34\textwidth}}
\caption{Core gateway use cases}\\
\toprule
\textbf{ID} & \textbf{Use case} & \textbf{Primary actor} & \textbf{System outcome} \\
\midrule
\endfirsthead
\toprule
\textbf{ID} & \textbf{Use case} & \textbf{Primary actor} & \textbf{System outcome} \\
\midrule
\endhead
UC001 & Onboard and activate merchant & Admin or Ops & Merchant profile, review evidence, access credentials, VietQR receiving account, and operational status are established before live transaction activity. \\
\bodyrowrule
UC002 & Create dynamic VietQR payment & Merchant backend & A pending payment request is created and a scannable VietQR payload, QR reference, and PNG image are returned for customer-facing checkout. \\
\bodyrowrule
UC003 & Process payment result callback & Provider callback source & Callback evidence is stored and the payment moves to a final state or to manual review when evidence is ambiguous. \\
\bodyrowrule
UC004 & Create full refund and process result & Merchant backend and provider callback source & A full refund request is created and later finalized from external confirmation. \\
\bodyrowrule
UC005 & Deliver webhook with retry and manual recovery & Scheduled trigger and internal operator & Merchant notification attempts are recorded, retries are controlled, and failed deliveries can be retried safely. \\
\bottomrule
\end{longtable}

\section{Dashboard Use Cases}

\begin{longtable}{L{0.11\textwidth}L{0.26\textwidth}L{0.18\textwidth}L{0.27\textwidth}}

\toprule
\textbf{ID} & \textbf{Use case} & \textbf{Actor} & \textbf{System outcome} \\
\midrule
\endhead
DUC001 & Sign in and view operations overview & Admin or Ops & Internal users access a consolidated operational view of merchants, transactions, delivery issues, and pending reviews. \\
\bodyrowrule
DUC002 & Manage merchant lifecycle & Admin or Ops & Internal users register merchants, review readiness information, manage credentials, configure QR receiving accounts, and apply lifecycle decisions. \\
\bodyrowrule
DUC003 & Manage merchant dashboard accounts & Admin or Ops & Merchant-facing user accounts can be created, updated, deactivated, reactivated, or reset. \\
\bodyrowrule
DUC004 & Inspect operational evidence & Admin or Ops & Internal users review payments, refunds, webhook history, reconciliation items, and audit evidence. \\
\bodyrowrule
DUC005 & Sign in and maintain account access & Merchant dashboard user & Merchant representatives authenticate securely and can change their own password. \\
\bodyrowrule
DUC006 & View overview and analytics & Merchant dashboard user & Merchant representatives view summarized activity and trend information for a selected reporting window. \\
\bodyrowrule
DUC007 & Explore payment, refund, and webhook records & Merchant dashboard user & Merchant representatives review transaction and notification history, including stored QR reference and QR payload evidence, that belongs only to their merchant account. \\
\bodyrowrule
DUC008 & View profile and credential metadata & Merchant dashboard user & Merchant representatives can read merchant profile information and masked credential details without seeing raw secrets. \\
\bottomrule
